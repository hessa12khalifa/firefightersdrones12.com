About Us :

It's one of those companies that cares about the community and safety, as well as the perspective of its customers, by offering to develop and produce a new idea to help firefighters solve the difficult problem they've been handed.Drones allow firefighters to quickly and effectively scout out dangerous fires, observe and monitor a large blaze and the surrounding area and more.




Our Purpose :

Firefighter Drones are dispatched as scouts to fire scenes, where they use cameras equipped with thermal imaging technology to aid first responders in their rescue operations. Drones with thermal cameras can see in low-light-dark circumstances and detect anomalies on diverse infrastructure, such as power lines.
